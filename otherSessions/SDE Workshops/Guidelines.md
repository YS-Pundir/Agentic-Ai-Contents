Workshops Roadmap – Key Guidelines


Plan for a total of 15 workshop sessions
Sessions must align with shared curriculum; avoid batch-specific dependency
Ensure relevance across batches; no session should feel out-of-context
Each session: 90% hands-on, max 10% theory
Follow the problem statement approach: build, analyze, and learn practical outcomes
Every session must be independent and executable standalone
Integrate AI/tools as per the curriculum—focus on practical usage and implementation
Difficulty progression: 10 beginner, 3–5 intermediate, 2 advanced sessions
Audience includes mixed batches at different stages; the design must accommodate all levels
Ensure real-world relevance and progressive learning across sessions
Please adhere to these guidelines while creating the roadmap
