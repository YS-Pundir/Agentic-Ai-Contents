topics be covered: Rise of LLMs; tokens; context windows; hallucinations (intro)


detailed subtopics to be covered:

* Describe hallucinations and other common failure modes in plain language with examples.
* Relate GenAI capabilities to prior ML ideas without requiring neural-network mathematics.
