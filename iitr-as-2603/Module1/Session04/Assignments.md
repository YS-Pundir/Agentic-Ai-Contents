### **Q1. What is the correct way to create an empty list in Python?**

A. `{}`
B. `[]`
C. `()`
D. `list{}`

**Answer:** B

---

### **Q2. Which of the following correctly creates a list from a range of numbers 0 to 4?**

A. `range(5)`
B. `list(0,5)`
C. `list(range(5))`
D. `[range(5)]`

**Answer:** C

---

### **Q3. What will be the output of the following?**

```python
items = [10, 20, 30, 40]
print(items[-1])
```

A. 10
B. 20
C. 30
D. 40

**Answer:** D

---

### **Q4. What does slicing `data[2:5]` return?**

A. Elements from index 2 to 5 (inclusive)
B. Elements from index 2 to 4
C. Elements from index 3 to 5
D. Error

**Answer:** B

---

### **Q5. What is the output of the following?**

```python
lst = [0, 1, 2, 3, 4]
print(lst[::2])
```

A. `[1, 3]`
B. `[0, 2, 4]`
C. `[0, 1, 2]`
D. `[2, 4]`

**Answer:** B

### **Q6. Which loop is most Pythonic for iterating through a list?**

A. `for i in range(len(lst))`
B. `while i < len(lst)`
C. `for item in lst`
D. `loop(lst)`

**Answer:** C
