| PCA Visualization – projecting high dimensional data into 2D for visualization | Covered | Motivation only: with many features you cannot visualize; humans limited past ~3D; reducing to two dimensions enables scatter-plot style views; aligns with the illustrative “many features → two features” narrative rather than a formal PCA projection/biplot workflow. |
