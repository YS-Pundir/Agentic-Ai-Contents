title: Introduction to Databases for AI Systems	

objective: Understand how databases store and organize data used by AI systems	

topics to be covered: relational databases;SQL basics;structured vs unstructured data	

detailed subtopics: 
- Understand the Role of Data in AI Systems — Explain how AI systems rely on data for training, inference, and decision-making, and why efficient storage is critical;
- Introduce the Concept of Databases — Define databases and compare them with file-based storage (CSV/Excel), highlighting scalability, consistency, and querying advantages;
- Explain Relational Databases — Introduce structured data storage using tables, and explain rows (records) and columns (attributes);
- Understand Tables and Basic Data Modeling — Show how real-world entities (users, products, students) are represented as tables with meaningful attributes;
- Introduce Keys and Relationships — Explain primary keys and foreign keys, and how relationships connect multiple tables into a structured system;
